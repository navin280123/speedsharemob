# Privacy Policy for SpeedShare

Last updated: June 23, 2026

SpeedShare values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how SpeedShare handles your information when you use our application to share files directly between devices over the same WiFi network.

## 1. Information Collection
SpeedShare does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, files, or telemetry data to any external server. All file transfers occur directly peer-to-peer between devices on your local WiFi network.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not use or share your information in any way.

## 3. File Transfers
All files shared using SpeedShare remain within your local network and are never uploaded to any cloud or third-party servers. You are responsible for ensuring that you trust the devices you are connecting to.

## 4. Security
We implement direct socket-based local transfer protocols to protect connections between devices; however, please ensure your local WiFi network is secure and you only connect to trusted devices.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the application and updated online.
